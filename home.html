About Us

The University of Washington Steel Bridge team is a student-led club that designs, fabricates, and constructs a 20-25 ft steel bridge and competes at the annual AISC Student Steel Bridge Competition. At its core, Steel Bridge concentrates on providing opportunities for young engineers to develop new skills and collaborate on an innovative project. The team strives to build an inclusive environment in which students of all experience levels are welcome.
